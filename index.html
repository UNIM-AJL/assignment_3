Midnight

We chose to call this collection "Midnight" because as soon as we opened the kiln door, the first thing that came to our mind is that the new glaze combination looks exactly like starry skies at midnight; Each vase turned out a bit different to the other, like each starry night is different than the one before.

We obviously could not resist naming the beautiful vases after the stars, each star name has an interesting meaning and history behind it.  ​

Our vases are great on their own, but even better when coupled together as a decorative piece, with or without flowers.
